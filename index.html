High Passes & Higher Vibes – Discover Ladakh

Get ready to experience Ladakh like never before! Whether you're riding through the rugged mountains on a bike or joining the journey in a comfy Traveller, this adventure covers it all: Leh, Khardung La, Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake, Hanle, Umling La, and more. From epic roads and dramatic landscapes to starry nights and unforgettable memories, this trip is built for those who crave both adventure and adventure only!

Ride through some of the world’s highest motorable passes, enjoy camel rides and ATVs in Nubra, and feel the calm magic of Pangong’s crystal-clear waters. Reach Umling La, sitting even higher than Everest Base Camp. This trip blends adventure, fun, and raw beauty into one epic experience. It’s not just a Ride, it’s the kind of journey you’ll remember forever.

Complete Trip Itinerary

  • Arrive at Leh airport after a stunning flight over the majestic Himalayas.
  • Meet Roamers representative who will assist you with a comfortable transfer to your hotel.
  • Complete the check-in process and unwind for a while.
  • Spend the rest of the day in leisure with the team.
  • Relax and take a stroll nearby to help your body acclimatize to the altitude.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.

  • Wake up to a refreshing morning in Leh and enjoy a hearty breakfast at the hotel to fuel up for the day.
  • Visit the iconic Shanti Stupa for panoramic views of Leh and the Himalayas.
  • Head toward Sham Valley, starting with the beautiful confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers at Sangam viewpoint.
  • Stop at Magnetic Hill, known for its fascinating optical illusion where vehicles appear to roll uphill.
  • Visit Pathar Sahib Gurudwara and learn about the legend of Guru Nanak Dev Ji.
  • Explore the Hall of Fame museum dedicated to Indian soldiers and their bravery.
  • Evening free to explore the vibrant Leh Market — shop for souvenirs and unwind at cozy cafés.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.

Distance: 125 km | Duration: 5–6 Hours
  • Start your day with a delicious breakfast at your hotel in Leh, get ready for the adventure ahead.
  • Gear up for an unforgettable drive as we begin our ascent along one of the highest motorable roads in the world.
  • Feel the thrill as we head towards Nubra Valley, nestled between the Karakoram and Ladakh ranges, offering dramatic views all around.
  • Our first major stop is Khardung-La Pass, sitting at a staggering 5,359 meters, where you'll have time to take in the sweeping mountain vistas.
  • We then descend along the route tracing the Shyok River, entering the stunning landscapes of Nubra Valley, also known as Ldorma or the Valley of Flowers.
  • Visit Diskit Monastery, home to a towering 106-foot statue of Maitreya Buddha overlooking the valley.
  • Explore the white-sand dunes of Hunder — a cold desert straight out of a dream.
  • Optional: Ride a double-humped Bactrian camel or enjoy an ATV ride (at your own cost).
  • After a full day of exploration, make your way to your campsite or hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

Distance: 160 km | Duration: 5–6 Hours
  • Wake up to the serene views of Nubra Valley and prepare for another adventurous drive.
  • Pass through Agam and Shyok villages, known for their untouched beauty and peaceful charm.
  • Catch the first stunning glimpses of Pangong Lake.
  • Located at 4,300 meters, Pangong is the world’s highest saltwater lake.
  • Marvel at its ever-changing blue hues and surrounding peaks.
  • Visit the famous "3 Idiots" movie spot for fun photos.
  • Check in at your lakeside campsite and enjoy a warm dinner.
  • Overnight stay near Pangong Lake.

Distance: 165 km | Duration: 8–9 Hours
  • Enjoy breakfast by the lake and prepare for an offbeat drive to Hanle.
  • Pass through Chushul and visit the historically rich Rezang La War Memorial.
  • Pay tribute to the 1962 Indo-China War heroes.
  • Lunch break en route depending on time/location.
  • Cross the scenic Loma Bridge over the Indus River.
  • Reach the remote village of Hanle by evening.
  • Check in to your welcoming homestay and enjoy a traditional dinner.
  • Overnight stay in Hanle.

Distance: approx. 150 km | Duration: 7–8 Hours
  • Enjoy breakfast at the homestay and prepare for a thrilling adventure to Umling La.
  • Cross Photi La Pass at 5,524 meters with surreal views.
  • Reach Umling La Pass — the highest motorable road in the world at 5,640 meters.
  • Admire the dramatic landscape and high-altitude beauty.
  • On return, cross Nurbu La Pass (5,282 meters).
  • Optional visit to Hanle Observatory if time permits.
  • Return to your homestay for a cozy dinner under the stars.
  • Overnight stay in Hanle.

Distance: approx. 260 km | Duration: 6–7 Hours
  • Wake early for a beautiful sunrise over the Ladakh mountains.
  • After breakfast, begin your scenic return journey to Leh.
  • Pass through Chumathang, Upshi, Karu, Thikshey, and Shey.
  • Arrive in Leh by evening and check in to your hotel.
  • Evening free to explore Leh market, shop, and relax at local cafés.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.
